Why Choose Us?

    • Experienced Therapists:

      Our team consists of licensed and compassionate therapists, experienced mentors, and psychologists, who will guide you through whatever steps you need to take.




    • Personalized Care:

      We prioritize your individual needs. Working closely with you to understand your specific needs, circumstances, goals, and crafting personalized treatment plans for your unique situation.




    • Confidentiality:

      Your privacy is our utmost priority. We assure that services are conducted in an ethical and professional manner. All contacts, records and sessions are kept strictly confidential. We strive to provide a safe and entirely confidential atmosphere promoting emotional and psychological health.




    • Convenient Scheduling:

      Our teletherapy options are designed to fit your busy lifestyle. We offer a 30 minute online informational visit, it involves a brief discussion of the major problems, how we propose to address these issues with you, payment plans, scheduling of sessions, and answers to general questions you have about the therapeutic process.

      When you schedule a consultation, someone will usually reach you within one hour, during business hours, 9am to 6:00 pm (WAT). Early morning, evening and weekend appointments may occasionally be available upon request. We do not offer crisis, walk-in or inpatient services of any type.




    • Data-Driven Insights:

      We utilize evidence-based practices and psychological principles for all our services.

Q: How do I know if therapy is right for me?

Answer: Therapy is for anyone seeking clarity, growth, or support. Whether you’re facing specific challenges or just want to improve your well-being, the first step is to let someone know you want help.


Q: Who do we see?

Answer: We see anyone with problems that may have become difficult to manage without guidance. We understand that mental health is not limited by age. That's why our services are individually tailored to provide comprehensive care for individuals of all age groups; children, adolescents, adults, individuals, couples and families.

Q: How do we help?

Answer: We provide a safe, caring, and confidential environment to discuss your problems. We guide you and your family based on our extensive professional experience to develop long term coping strategies; we do not offer a ‘quick fix’ for problems, but personalized treatment plans delivered weekly for 8 to 16 weeks to address most issues.
