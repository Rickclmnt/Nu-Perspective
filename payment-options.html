Payment Options

We offer secure and flexible payment options to make it easy for you to complete your session booking.

Online Payment (Paystack)

Our primary payment method is Paystack, a trusted and secure platform that allows you to pay with your debit or credit card, bank account, or USSD. Once your payment is confirmed, your booking will be automatically registered.

Gift or Sponsored Session

You can also sponsor a session for someone else. Simply provide their name and email when completing payment or transfer details. We’ll notify them once the session is confirmed.
